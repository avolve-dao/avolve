# Avolve Platform User Guide

This guide explains how to engage with the Avolve platform's regenerative gamified system, including subscribing, participating through daily claims, contributing, and unlocking features through metrics improvements.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the Avolve platform at [https://avolve.io](https://avolve.io)
2. Click on "Sign Up" in the top-right corner
3. Complete the registration form with your email, password, and basic profile information
4. Verify your email address through the confirmation link
5. Complete your profile setup to receive your initial GEN tokens

### Navigating the Dashboard

After logging in, you'll be directed to your dashboard, which provides an overview of:

- Your token balances across all categories
- Available daily claims
- Unlocked and locked features
- Current challenges and progress
- Metrics and achievements

## Subscribing with GEN

GEN is the primary token of the Avolve ecosystem, representing the Supercivilization pillar. Subscribing with GEN provides access to premium features and contributes to the ecosystem's growth.

### Subscription Options

1. **Basic Membership**: 10 GEN per month
   - Access to basic features and daily claims
   - Participation in community discussions
   - Limited access to learning resources

2. **Premium Membership**: 50 GEN per month
   - Full access to all platform features
   - Priority access to events and workshops
   - Advanced analytics and personalized recommendations
   - Voting rights in governance decisions

3. **Founder Membership**: 100 GEN per month
   - All Premium benefits
   - Early access to new features
   - Direct influence on platform development
   - Special recognition in the community

### How to Subscribe

1. Navigate to the "Membership" section in your profile
2. Select your desired subscription level
3. Complete the payment process (fiat or crypto)
4. Your subscription will be activated immediately, and your GEN tokens will be allocated to your wallet

Remember that when you contribute financially, your fiat is allocated as follows:
- 10% to GEN (Supercivilization)
- 40% to SAP (Superachiever)
- 50% to SCQ (Superachievers)

## Participating with Daily Claims

The platform follows a weekly schedule for token claims, with each day dedicated to a specific token. Regular participation through daily claims is essential for progressing in your journey and unlocking features.

### Daily Claim Schedule

| Day | Token | Focus Area |
|-----|-------|------------|
| Sunday | SPD | Superpuzzle Developments |
| Monday | SHE | Superhuman Enhancements |
| Tuesday | PSP | Personal Success Puzzle |
| Wednesday | SSA | Supersociety Advancements |
| Thursday | BSP | Business Success Puzzle |
| Friday | SGB | Supergenius Breakthroughs |
| Saturday | SMS | Supermind Superpowers |

### How to Claim Daily Tokens

1. Log in to the platform on the designated day
2. Navigate to the "Daily Claim" section on your dashboard
3. Complete the daily micro-challenge or reflection question
4. Click "Claim [TOKEN]" to receive your tokens
5. Tokens will be immediately added to your wallet

### Claim Streaks and Bonuses

Maintaining a consistent claim streak provides additional benefits:

- **3-Day Streak**: 10% bonus tokens on your next claim
- **7-Day Streak**: 25% bonus tokens and a special badge
- **30-Day Streak**: 50% bonus tokens and exclusive access to limited features

## Unlocking Features through Metrics

The platform uses a metrics-driven approach to feature unlocking. By improving your engagement and participation metrics, you can unlock additional features and capabilities.

### Key Metrics

- **DAU/MAU Ratio**: How frequently you engage with the platform
- **Retention**: How consistently you return to the platform
- **ARPU**: Your financial contribution to the ecosystem
- **NPS**: Your satisfaction and likelihood to recommend the platform
- **Engagement**: Your level of activity and participation

### Feature Unlock Requirements

1. **Teams Feature**
   - Complete 10 challenges
   - Unlocks the ability to create or join teams for collaborative work

2. **Governance Feature**
   - Accumulate 100 GEN tokens
   - Unlocks the ability to create petitions and vote on platform decisions

3. **Marketplace Feature**
   - Complete 20 challenges OR achieve DAU/MAU > 0.3
   - Unlocks the ability to buy, sell, and trade items with other users

4. **Token Utility Feature**
   - Complete 5 challenges AND claim 3 different day tokens
   - Unlocks the ability to spend tokens for premium features

### Tracking Your Progress

1. Navigate to the "Features" section on your dashboard
2. View your current metrics and progress toward unlocking features
3. See specific requirements for each locked feature
4. Receive recommendations for improving your metrics

## Maximizing Your Experience

### Best Practices

1. **Daily Engagement**
   - Log in daily to claim your tokens
   - Complete at least one micro-challenge each day
   - Engage with community content regularly

2. **Balanced Participation**
   - Distribute your activity across all three value pillars
   - Claim tokens on different days of the week
   - Participate in both individual and collective activities

3. **Metrics Optimization**
   - Focus on improving metrics that align with features you want to unlock
   - Track your progress regularly and adjust your activity accordingly
   - Celebrate milestones and achievements to maintain motivation

### Common Questions

**Q: What happens if I miss a daily claim?**
A: You can only claim tokens on their designated day. If you miss a claim, you'll need to wait until the following week for that specific token.

**Q: How do I check my metrics?**
A: Your metrics are displayed on your dashboard under the "Metrics" section. You can view detailed breakdowns and historical trends.

**Q: Can I transfer tokens to other users?**
A: Yes, you can transfer tokens to other users through the "Wallet" section. Different tokens may have different transfer restrictions.

**Q: How do I unlock features faster?**
A: Focus on completing challenges, maintaining consistent daily engagement, and contributing to community initiatives to improve your metrics and unlock features more quickly.
