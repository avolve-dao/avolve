## Token Hierarchy

The Avolve platform has a hierarchical token structure that aligns with its three main value pillars:

```
GEN (Supercivilization) - Ecosystem journey
├── SAP (Superachiever) - Individual journey
│   ├── PSP (Personal Success Puzzle)
│   ├── BSP (Business Success Puzzle)
│   └── SMS (Supermind Superpowers)
└── SCQ (Superachievers) - Collective journey

    └── SGB (Supergenius Breakthroughs)
```

## Meeting Structure (Fractally Governance)

Each weekly meeting follows the Fractally governance approach:

1. **Check-in** (5-10 minutes)
   - Participants share their current state and intentions for the meeting
   - Focus on the specific token's area of concern

2. **Group Formation** (5 minutes)
   - Participants self-organize into groups of 5-6 people
   - Joshua Seymour (as benevolent dictator) has final say on group composition

3. **Consensus Building** (30-45 minutes)
   - Groups discuss token allocation proposals
   - Use Fibonacci-weighted voting to reach consensus
   - Document decisions and rationale

4. **Integration** (15-20 minutes)
   - Group representatives share consensus decisions
   - Joshua Seymour reviews and can veto or modify decisions
   - Final allocations are recorded and implemented

5. **Check-out** (5 minutes)
   - Participants share key takeaways and commitments
   - Confirm next meeting details

## Token Distribution

Token distribution follows these principles:

1. **Individual Contributions**
   - PSP, BSP, and SMS tokens reward individual contributions
   - Allocated based on leaderboard ranking and specific achievements

2. **Collective Contributions**
   - SPD, SHE, SSA, and SGB tokens reward collective/team contributions
   - Allocated based on team performance and consensus decisions

3. **Ecosystem Growth**
   - GEN tokens represent overall ecosystem contribution
   - Allocated by Joshua Seymour based on exceptional contributions to all areas

## Implementation Notes

- Initially, focus on individual leaderboard and contribution tracking
- As the community grows, gradually introduce team formation and collective rewards
- Joshua Seymour maintains ultimate authority throughout the transition to full Fractally governance
