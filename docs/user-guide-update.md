The `JourneyProgress` component on your dashboard provides real-time visibility into your regenerative transformation:

![Journey Progress Component](/assets/images/journey-progress-component.png)

- **Instant Token Earnings**: Watch your tokens appear in your wallet the moment you complete a challenge—no waiting, no uncertainty
- **Streak Visualization**: See your Tesla 3-6-9 streak pattern building in real-time, with clear indicators of your multiplier status
- **Milestone Celebrations**: Experience micro-celebrations when you reach key milestones, reinforcing your progress
- **Phase Transitions**: Receive clear notifications when you advance from Discovery to Onboarding, Scaffolding, and Endgame phases

Each interaction with the platform is designed to provide immediate feedback, ensuring you never wonder if your actions are making a difference. Your time investment is respected through instant recognition and rewards.
